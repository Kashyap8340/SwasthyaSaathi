About SwasthyaSaathi AI

SwasthyaSaathi AI is a digital public health companion that provides reliable healthcare awareness through AI-powered conversations. We bridge the gap between complex medical information and everyday health literacy.

Always here to help

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower every individual with the knowledge they need to lead healthier lives. By leveraging advanced technology, we strive to improve health awareness, ensure information reliability, and promote preventive care through accessible, conversational interfaces.

The Problem We Are Solving

Addressing the critical gaps in modern public healthcare accessibility.

Limited Access

Overburdened healthcare systems and lack of doctors.

Lack of Awareness

Poor knowledge of symptoms and preventive measures.

Language Barriers

Non-English speakers struggle with health info.

Misinformation

The spread of unverified medical myths.

Missed Vaccines

Forgotten schedules leading to preventable diseases.

Our Solution

AI Health Guidance

Intelligent conversational interface providing verified public health information instantly.

Multilingual Support

Breaking barriers with support for English, Hindi, and Odia to reach every heart and home.

Omnichannel Availability

Accessible across Web, WhatsApp, and SMS for seamless interaction anywhere.
