# SwasthyaSaathi AI

## 1. Introduction
**SwasthyaSaathi AI** is an AI-powered public health awareness platform designed to improve access to reliable healthcare information for people, especially in **rural and semi-urban communities**.

The platform acts as a **digital health companion** that provides instant guidance about diseases, preventive healthcare, vaccination schedules, and outbreak alerts through **AI-based conversations**.

Users can interact with SwasthyaSaathi AI through multiple channels such as:
- Web portal
- WhatsApp chatbot
- SMS messaging

This multi-channel approach ensures that people with **limited internet access or digital literacy** can still receive important health information.

## 2. Problem the Platform Solves
Many communities face several healthcare challenges:
- Limited access to doctors and medical facilities
- Lack of awareness about disease symptoms and prevention
- Language barriers in understanding health information
- Spread of misinformation about diseases and vaccines
- Missed vaccination schedules
- Late awareness of disease outbreaks

Because of these problems, people often seek medical help **only after the condition becomes severe**.
SwasthyaSaathi AI aims to **bridge the gap between healthcare systems and citizens** by delivering timely, understandable health guidance.

## 3. Core Idea
The core idea behind SwasthyaSaathi AI is to create an **AI assistant that provides preventive healthcare awareness and guidance**. Instead of diagnosing diseases, the AI focuses on:
- Explaining symptoms
- Providing preventive health advice
- Educating users about diseases
- Encouraging early medical consultation

This helps people make **better health decisions at the right time**.

## 4. Key Features
- **AI Health Chatbot:** Conversational AI chatbot that answers health-related questions in simple language.
- **Multilingual Support:** English, Hindi, and Odia.
- **Disease Awareness:** Information about common diseases (Dementia, UTI, Viral Fever, Dengue, Malaria) including symptoms, causes, prevention, and when to seek help.
- **Vaccination & Medicine Reminders:** SMS and email notifications for child vaccination, pregnancy vaccines, and medicine intake.
- **Real-Time Outbreak Alerts:** Dengue outbreak warnings, heatwave advisories, flu season alerts.
- **Multi-Platform Accessibility:** Web Portal, WhatsApp Chatbot, and SMS Chatbot.

## 5. Target Users
- Rural citizens, elderly people, parents, pregnant women, and youth.
- Community health workers and public health organizations.

## 6. Technology Stack
- **AI & NLP:** For conversational responses trained on trusted public health data.
- **Frontend Stack:** HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript (WCAG Compliant, Mobile-First).
- **Messaging APIs:** For SMS and WhatsApp integrations.

## 7. Social Impact & Vision
SwasthyaSaathi AI aims to improve public health by increasing disease awareness, promoting preventive healthcare practices, reducing misinformation, and encouraging early medical consultation. The long-term vision is to become a **nationwide digital health awareness assistant** integrated with government health systems.

## 🚀 Getting Started (Landing Page UI)
A modern, accessible healthcare-themed landing page is included in this repository.

### How to Use Local UI
1. **Open in Browser**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Deploy**: Copy the files to your web server
3. **Customize**: Edit the HTML/CSS to update branding, contact info, and colors.

### Customization
Edit the CSS variables in the `<style>` tag:
```css
:root {
    --primary-blue: #0066cc;
    --dark-blue: #004499;
    --light-blue: #e6f2ff;
    --accent-gold: #ffc107;
}
```
